What this mockup is showing: the "Falstad magic" — a circuit where you can see current as moving dots and feel cause-and-effect (more resistance → slower dots, dimmer LED). The real feature would solve the circuit properly (nodal analysis) for any components you place, and the Ohmlet tutor would narrate it live and tie it to your lessons and your real bench. Three ways to build it (embed the GPL CircuitJS1, fork it, or build our own engine) are written up in metadata/falstad-simulator-research.md.
